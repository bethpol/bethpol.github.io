About My Films

I've been writing, directing, acting, and editing narritive, PSA, comedies, and documentary films since 2014. Most of my filmmaking experience comes from NJ Film School, where I've taken classes for years. This portfolio includes some of my best works, including a number of film festival-winning shorts.
