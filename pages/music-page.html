About My Music

I've been composing, playing, and enjoying music for 13+ years starting off with beginning piano lessons at age 3. At age 10, I began playing cello, which I have continued to play in the Ridge High School Orchestra as well as in chamber ensembles. In high school, I broadened my musical horizons by joining the Ridge Marching Band, and currently play as a percussionist in the pit. My instrumental focus is currently harp. I study irish, contemporary, and classical music and have played with the Ridge High School Wind Ensemble.

I began composing music since fourth grade for string ensemble, orchestra, and harp ensemble. Stay tuned for audio clips of my compositions!

For the past 2 years, I have been an inducted member of the Tri-M National Music Honors Society, and will serve as an officer for my school's branch in the 2019-20 school year.
