Flight Computer PCB

Cornell Rocketry Team Project - Fall 2022

My Fall 2022 Design project was to develop the Central Flight Computer for our 2023 competition rocket, Ursa Major. This rocket will be launched at the Spaceport America Cup in the 10,000 foot target apogee division. For my project, I developed a PCB using Altium design software that integrates the RP2040 chip with peripherals including an SD card, flash memory, a LoRa radio module, and pin headers to allow it to interface with other electrical systems. This board will run the central flight code loop that will trigger mission critical events during flight based off of sensor data.

I started the process by creating schematics based upon the datasheets for each of the components. I them created the PCB design, and routed all of the connections. Finally, I ran design review checks and simulations to ensure intended functionality, and ordered the board for fabrication.
